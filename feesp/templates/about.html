About FeePay....

The Online Fees Payment portal is to make a Fees payment by online instead of making a payment throuth long queue in office, It takes more time, To avoiding this to make a payment on online it saves time.

The Portal contains Two types of fees payment option one is Exam Fees Payment and another one is Academic fees payment. In the Exam Fees payment students getting the exam fees form to filling this form they can make payment. After filling the exam fees form that is redirect to the college provided Exam Fees form So, students do not worry about the form and also same as the Academic Fees Payment by the same procedure by online.The staff need not take headache to receiving payment amount from the students.

This portal also provides the Exam fees details and Academic Fees details like In exam fees payment students can will know subject code for each suject,amount for each subject, and also backlog subjects So, getting this details students can pay current semester fees and coming to academic fees payment students can know the amount for each Courses to knowing this can pay the Academic fees from home by Online and also this portal provides the Installment facility to the students so they do not worry about that. After making a payment students can get fees receipt and they can save and print the receipt.
This portal also provides the Feedback option In that the students can give suggestions to this portal and satisfaction about this portal.
