APPLY FOR CONCESSION.........

The students can apply for concession over their fees. There are three types of concession available for the students. If eligible you can apply for any one of the following concessions.
Upload the required document and after verification, you would receive mail about your concession application and then you can avail the specified concession. After aproval of your concession proceed ahead to pay your academic fees. :-

Girls student Concession

  • Empowering the girls' education by providing concession of {{conc2}}%

    Required Document:
    Scanned copy of Aadhar card or Birth certificate

Scholarship for 90% and above

  • If you have scored 90% or above agregate in last year.Then you can avail {{conc1}}% off on your current acamedic year fees.

    Required Document:
    Previous year marks card indicating that you have scored above 90% in academics.
    One Document containing both the semester's markcard should be uploades.

SC/ST Concession

  • Students of Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribes can avail concession of {{conc3}}%

    Required Document:
    Scanned copy of Caste Certificate


Note**** you can only apply for any one of the above scholarships
